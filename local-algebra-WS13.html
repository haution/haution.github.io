Olivier Haution

Content:

This course is centered on the applications of techniques of homological algebra to commutative algebra. Using only the language of commutative algebra, one can define the notion of a regular local ring, and ask the following questions:
  • is the localisation of regular local ring again regular?
  • is a regular local ring a unique factorisation domain?
One can show, using homological methods, that these questions have a positive answer; this constitutes one the great success of homological algebra.
The main topics covered will be: depth, Cohen-Macaulay modules, Serre's definition of a regular rings, Auslander-Buchsbaum formula, factorial rings, Gorenstein rings.
